About

The concept of our project is to create an application and a website about the Innovations in Healthcare and to give the opportunity to hospitals to use electricity generated by solar panels. We used algorithmic approaches to decide whether this source of renewable energy is suitable for health centers depending on their circumstances!

A solar panel is a device that converts sunlight into electricity by harnessing the photovoltaic effect. It consists of interconnected solar cells, typically made of semiconductor materials.

Utilizing solar panels, hospitals could power their equipment with ecological and renewable energy. By making use of solar energy, we eliminate our carbon footprint which also lowers pollution levels and therefore leads to less disease and sicknesses.

Solar energy, in the form of sunlight, is a very powerful source of renewable energy, however, only a small percentage is utilized by our civilization.
